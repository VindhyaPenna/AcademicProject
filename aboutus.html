KRISHNA CHAITANYA EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS

Krishna Chaitanya College has carved a niche for itself over a period of two decades. The milestone for Krishna Chaitanya College was laid by a two young men Dr.RV Krishna Reddy and P.Chandra Sekhar Reddy. In the Year 1998, Krishna Chaitanya College was started with three departments, 140 students and limited faculty in Nellore, Andhra Pradesh. Since then it is constantly progressing in motivating students and staff engaged in active learning through the best use of modern technology. Krishna Chaitanya College is not just an educational institution, it is a commitment, towards building a new generation of smart and capable individuals. It not only provides quality Education, but teaches them the right attitude towards life. One among the top colleges in our state. Krishna Chaitanya College has wide reputation for holistic approach towards grooming the students. Today, Krishna Chaitanya happens to be one of the largest educational groups in the state of Andhra Pradesh with more than 300 faculty members and around 200 administrative staff and above 10000 students. Krishna Chaitanya is known for establishing unique traditions in every aspects of its functioning.

Krishna chaitanya has spread its wings through most sections of education like intermediate,degree,pg

Krishna Chaitanya junior college was established in 2006 with an aim to provide Quality education to the students of Nellore. With it’s suspendous success and repeated request from the student Community and enthusiasm parents have prompted them to start a Residential campus. The institution started Residential junior College exclusively for Boys in Wahabpet (prabhanjana campus) and Residential junior college exclusively for girls in wahabpet (vasishta campus). Later with an ardent view the institution was expanded in different areas. Since then it has been attracting the students. Every Care is taken to ensure students success in Intermediate and competitive exams like IIT, JEE, AIEEE, BITSAT & NEET both at the same time. Certain teaching procedure are moulded considering the standard of students and their goals.

Our Academic System :
The academic council of the college consists of compartments Heads and academic supervisors with the principal as the team leader. The council meets every team to plan our strategies for better performance of the teachers, materials, infra-structure and program for effective methodology. The whole years plan concerning academics are analyzed, discussed and finalized in the Academic council. Here Knowledge imparting will not help the students to achieve the ultimate goal. Hence the college has deviced many systems to equip the students to achieve their ambition. Our Uniqueness is weekly unit test i.e., Every tuesday and saturday, unit tests are conducted in the first two periods of forenoon session and afternoon session. Students get ample oppurtunities for learning this weekly lessons then and there and prepare well for final examinations.

FACILITIES INCLUDES:

LABORATORIES:

The campuses of Krishna Chaitanya offers Physics,Chemistry,Botany.Zoology&Maths labs. Students gain practical experience through demonstrations and experiments. The students learn under the expert guidance of respective subject lecturers.

HOSTEL:

Krishna chaitanya hostels are established with the most modern facilities for students coming from far off places. Krishna Chaitanya serves the Students Efficiently. Krishna Chaitanya faculty supervises the Study Hours of hostels regularly. Separate hostels for boys and girls which are termed as best in Andhra Pradesh are available to students Preferring Hostel Studies. The hostel is under the supervision of Hostel-in-charge, Assistant hostel-in-charges and wardens. The hostel staff attends to students and ensures the amenities offered to them are in good order. Students are provided with mineral water for drinking and hot water for bathing.S

FOOD SERVICES:

Care is taken to prepare food under extremely hygienic conditions. Students are offered milk in the morning and milk/tea in the evening break along with snacks, apart from the regular breakfast, lunch and dinner. Food is tasted by the administrative staff before it is served to students to ensure Quality and proper cooking.

HEALTH CARE:

A Qualified doctor attends to the healthcare of students. Free medicine is given to a student who falls sick. Parents are kept informed of the health status of students who are indisposed.

TRANSPORT:

The College owns 5 vehicles which cave a distance of 20km radius for pick up and Drop of students. Safety of Students being ferried in the vehicles is our prime concern.
